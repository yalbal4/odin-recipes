Braised Cabbage

Description

This braised cabbage is easy to make, healthy, and tastes good. For these reasons, I eat this nearly every day. I don't get tired of it because it tastes great, and each time I make it, I change up the recipe a little bit.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Additional vegetables (beans, eggplant, squash)
  • Additional seasonings (soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, paprika, chile flakes)

Steps

  1. Roughly chop your vegetables, make sure the denser vegetables are chopped smaller, so everything can cook evenly
  2. Heat oil in pan, dump vegetables in
  3. Add salt and other seasonings
  4. Sweat the vegetables, stirring when needed
  5. Turn off the heat once the vegetables get some nice color
