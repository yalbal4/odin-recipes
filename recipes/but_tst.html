Buttered Toast

Description

This buttered toast recipe is pretty standard. It's simple and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Additional toppings (honey, salt, pepper, get crazy with it)

Steps

  1. Toast bread (use a pan, a toaster, the oven, or whatever else you got)
  2. Spread butter on that toast
  3. Add your other toppings, then enjoy buttery toast
