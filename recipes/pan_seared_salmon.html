Pan Seared Salmon

Description

This pan seared salmon is easy and tasty. I don't like cooking, but I really like eating, so this recipe strikes a nice balance between not having to spend a lot of time cooking, but the end result is really good. Also, this picture I found looks really good, but it's massive.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Any other seasonings you enjoy with salmon (I like soy sauce and seaweed powder)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan until shimmering
  2. Turn heat down a little and add salmon skin side down
  3. Sear salmon until mostly cooked through
  4. Flip salmon and let cook for an additional minute (not too long because you're mostly done at this point)
  5. Remove salmon from pan and plate it!
